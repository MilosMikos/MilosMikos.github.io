Helping Us :

Hi, if you're here it's certainly because you feel like you could help us in some way, either you're a translator, or a webdesigner, heck even a programmer. But there are many more ways to contribute. Notably reading the comics and reporting bugs. Or even, the filthiest one of all ! Posting memes in the discord. We would be glad to welcome you there.
